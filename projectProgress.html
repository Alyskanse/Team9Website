Design Process

In our first sketch, we wanted to create a book that had questions on each page and after the reader picked answer A or B, they would collect their piece

In our second sketch, we decided on what kind of book we wanted to make and drew a more detailed picture of how we would set up the questions and the pockets that would hold the puzzle pieces.

In our third sketch, we completely changed our idea of what we wanted our artifact to be. Instead of making a book,, which had no connection to our theme, we decided to have a glass jar with water that represented the last melted ice from Earth and plastic bags of the questions and puzzle pieces.
