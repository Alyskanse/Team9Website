Our First Prototype

A Description of our Artifact

The idea behind our artifact is that in the next 50 years, climate change will get much worse. This will lead to all the ice melting from the arctic regions, based on the fact that temperatures have already been rising there a lot in our current world. We had the idea at first to make a game where the player had to answer questions about climate change and make choices about what to do in order to help the world. We decided that these questions would be placed in a class jar or bottle with water in it. The water represents the last melted ice from Earth. At the start of the game the player reads instructions about how to play. In those instructions is A a QR code that will lead them to a digital twine game. Once they pick their answer to each question on the paper, they will go to the twine game and it will explain to them why that was a good or bad answer. Also after they pick an answer they will get a puzzle piece from the bottle. Once they have finished answering all the questions and collecting all the puzzle pieces, the puzzle will create a code that they will enter into the twine game and it will give the player a story of what happens to the world based on the answers that they chose.

Backstory

By 2019, the global average temperature raised by 0.50c. In the next 50 years, the temperature in the arctic region will rise by 4C to 7C. Among them, the temperature changes in the arctic regions will be the most significant. In the past 50 years, the arctic temperatures rose from 3 ℃ to 4 ℃. There are many factors contributing to global warming that scientists are still unable to fully explain.

Now in 2078, because of global warming, the earth's temperature is accelerating, and we don’t have four seasons anymore, only hot weather. The younger generations have never seen natural ice and snow. Because of the rising temperatures, there is less and less water on the earth. A climate research team found samples from scientists 58 years ago, and that is the melted ice that is left in this bottle that you are holding.

But now there is hope for the world since you have picked up this bottle. It is your time to save the world. You are about to embark on a game that could change the fate of the world for the better, or worse. You will be asked questions and depending on your answers, something will change. Be careful because the choices you make will affect the state of the world in the future.

How We Made It

The Physical Artifact

For the physical part of our artifact we found a jar to put the water in (the last melted ice) and put our printed out questions in with the corresponding puzzle pieces. In the jar we also have our instructions page, which tells the player how to play the game and gives them the QR code to the twine game.

The Digital Portion

For the digital part of our artifact we made a twine game that the player goes through while answering the questions. The player will read the questions from the jar, then go to the twine game to pick their answer. Once they click A or B for their answer it leads them to a page that tells them if that was a good or bad answer.
